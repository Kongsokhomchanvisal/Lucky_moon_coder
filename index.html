THE BROWN BEAR

About Brown Bears

The brown bear (Ursus arctos) is native to parts of the nothern Eurasia and North America. Its conservation status is currently least concern.

There are many subspecies within the brown bear species, including the Atlas bear and the Himalayan brown bear
